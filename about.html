About Us

At Rural Healthcare Emergency Response, we believe in empowering rural communities by improving access to healthcare during emergencies. Developed by Rishu Nagar and Asmita Sonal, this platform aims to address the unique healthcare challenges faced in rural areas, where medical infrastructure is often limited. Our mission is to ensure that no life is lost due to lack of timely healthcare information or resources. With our easy-to-navigate website and AI-powered chatbot, we strive to provide essential assistance when it's needed the most.
